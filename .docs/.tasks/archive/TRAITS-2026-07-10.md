## ┬¦0 ┬Ę Traits are pure granters

**A trait GRANTS things; it never carries the granted thing's stats, procs, or effects itself.**
The granted object is defined in its own database file and is the single source of truth for
everything it does. A trait that circumvents this (stamping a proc or a resistance directly)
invalidates the data-driven flow ŌĆö the same weapon would behave differently depending on which trait
granted it, and effects double-apply.

| `kind` | The trait declares | The granted object carries the effects | Defined in |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `stat` | `effects: {strengthBonusŌĆ”}` ŌĆö flat core-stat deltas ONLY, baked at pawn-gen | *(exception: stat traits ARE the direct channel for core stats)* | `traits.jsonc` |
| `attribute` | `effects: {workSpeed/workYield/workQuality, combatMods, nightVision, healRate}` ŌĆö derived-stat multipliers ONLY | *(exception: attribute traits ARE the direct channel for derived stats)* ŌĆö **never a resistance** (┬¦0a), never a core-stat rider | `traits.jsonc` ŌåÆ applied by `PawnStatService` |
| `bodyMod` | `bodyMods: [{target, hpMult?, weightKg?}]` + `grafts: [{limb, parts}]` | a grafted limb is a REAL limb ŌĆö its parts, HP, weapons-mounting, and armor share come from the part defs | `limbmap.jsonc` |
| `naturalGear` | `naturalWeapons: [itemId]` ┬Ę `naturalArmor`/`armorMods` ┬Ę `blocksSlots` ┬Ę `carryPenalty` ┬Ę `stage`/`evolvesTo` | the WEAPON item carries damage/type/reach/crit/**onHitCondition** ŌĆö the only proc source; armor magnitude distributes over the limbmap `armor` shares | `items.jsonc` (weapons) ┬Ę `limbmap.jsonc` (armor shape) |
| `passive` | `selfCondition` ┬Ę `aura: {condition, radius, affects}` ┬Ę `triggeredCondition` | the CONDITION def carries every modifier, resistance, trigger edge, and `activateWhen` gate | `conditions.jsonc` |
| `wound` | `wounds: [{part, severity, type?}]` | the wound type's bleed/pain/heal behaviour comes from the wound def; ┬¦0b for permanency | `wounds.jsonc` |

**Forbidden on any trait:** `onHitCondition` (procs live on the weapon item / condition),
`weaponBonus`, resistance keys in `effects`, and any stat/effect rider on a granter kind
(`bodyMod`/`naturalGear`/`passive`/`wound`). One primary `kind` per trait; a legendary bundle
composes several via `subCapabilities` (each sub-cap obeys its own kind's contract).

## ┬¦1 ┬Ę Taxonomy rules (ADR-028)

- **Strict kind separation** ŌĆö `stat` and `attribute` never mix; a granter kind carries no effects.
  Enforced by `traitRegistry.test.ts`.
- **Naming law** ŌĆö a `stat`/`attribute` trait's name must be mundane + generic; only body-touching
  kinds (`bodyMod`/`naturalGear`/`passive`/`wound`) may name anatomy (claw/fang/hide/eye/jointŌĆ”).
  Enforced by `ANATOMY_NAME_RE`. Descriptions and flavor lines follow the same rule for abstract kinds.
- **Scope** ŌĆö `racial` (physiology, drawn from the race pool) vs `personal` (temperament, any pawn).
- **Physique gates** ŌĆö `requires: {minWeightKg/maxHeightCm/minBuildŌĆ”}` blocks contradictory draws
  (Gaunt never lands on a 250 kg mass).
- **Reserved kinds (Phase 2, TODO):** `behavioral`, `needs` (blood/heat meters), `transformation`
  (werewolf/mermaid trigger sets).

## ┬¦2 ┬Ę Rarity budget & selection

Rarity (`rarities.jsonc` scale + `mythic` between epic and legendary) is a BUDGET on breadth:

| Rarity | Budget |
| --- | --- |
| `negative` | pure-downside FLAW ŌĆö drawn as a Gaussian **count** (Žā=1.25, clamp 0ŌĆō4: most pawns 0ŌĆō1, a 4-flaw wretch ~1/170), never race identity |
| common / uncommon | 1ŌĆō2 attribute categories, trade-offs allowed |
| rare | Ōēż1 attribute category **or** 1 capability |
| epic | Ōēż2 categories or capability + proc |
| mythic / legendary | bundles (`subCapabilities`, each rolled per pawn) |

Selection: each race rolls identity (one cumulative rarity roll, rarest first: legendary 1.5% ┬Ę
mythic 1.5% ┬Ę epic 3% ┬Ę rare 9%) + a small mundane pool; each pawn draws Ōēż2 racial + Ōēż3 personal
at spawn. Core-stat ladders: ┬▒1/┬▒3/┬▒5 = common/uncommon/rare; two-stat swings climb to epic;
all-stats +1/+3/+5 = epic/mythic/legendary (the dark mirror rides the `Blighted` heritage banner).

## ┬¦3 ┬Ę Natural gear model (ADR-029)

- Gear fields live ON the trait (`naturalWeapons`/`naturalArmor`/`armorMods`), NOT behind
  `selfCondition` (that older routing is dead ŌĆö a `selfCondition` now always means a dynamic/passive
  condition).
- **Weapons** are anatomy-gated for free: the item id must appear in a surviving limbmap part's
  `weapons` list (lose the jaw ŌåÆ lose the bite; both hands gone ŌåÆ thrash fallback). The ITEM is the
  single source for damage, type, reach, crit, and its `onHitCondition` proc.
- **Armor** is per-part: the plan's `armor` shares set the shape, the trait's magnitude sets the
  strength; `mode` stack/replace vs worn gear; `carryPenalty` (fraction of carry capacity, clamped
  so a bare pawn is never encumbered) is the cost.
- **Staging** ŌĆö 3-stage lines linked by `stage: 1|2|3` + `evolvesTo` chains (claws, fangs, horns,
  tusks, venom, breath, talons, beaks; hide/fur/chitin/feather armor lines). Stage weapons are
  separate items so each stage's numbers live on its item.
- **Utility gear** ŌĆö natural apparatus with a slot cost and a benefit instead of defense (wings ŌåÆ
  moveSpeed + back slot; spinnerets ŌåÆ web shot; gill frillsŌĆ”), losable with the host part.
- **Grafts (┬¦bodyMod)** ŌĆö a trait may graft limbs/parts from the global `PART_DEF_MAP` (wings, beak,
  tail) onto the humanoid tree; a grafted limb is hittable, losable, and hosts the line's weapon.
  Pawns compose bodies ├Ā la carte ŌĆö there is no whole-plan "-kin" swap.

## ┬¦4 ┬Ę Passive model

- `selfCondition` ŌĆö a permanent or `activateWhen`-gated condition; the condition def carries ALL
  modifiers/resistances (this is the legitimate home for affinity resistances, per ┬¦0a).
- `aura: {condition, radius, affects, lingerSeconds}` ŌĆö throttled (~3 s cadence) radius stamp with a
  linger tail; heritage-gated, one mutual-exclusion group (Ōēż1 aura per pawn), finite radius always.
- `triggeredCondition` ŌĆö meter-edge one-shots (pain ŌåÆ berserk).
- Trigger-conditioned traits (wet/cold/hot/dark/pain/bloodlossŌĆ”) are pure data: the condition graph's
  `activateWhen`/`triggers` predicates (conditions.jsonc) do the work.

## ┬¦5 ┬Ę Wound traits

Real permanent injuries stamped at generation (`applyTraitWounds`): scars Ōēż10% of the limb's maxHP
by severity, destroyed = lost part; paired parts flip sides per pawn; vital/critical parts refused
(never lethal at spawn). Scars are uncareable (`isUncareable`) ŌĆö they don't trigger Rest, caretakers
ignore them, and `recomputeWound` carries `permanent` forward. Elemental variants via wound `type`
(cut/burn/frostbite/crush) read as "old Ō¤©typeŌ¤® scar". ┬¦0b will move the permanency flag into data.

## ┬¦7 ┬Ę Deferred tail ŌĆö consciously left open (2026-07-10, at archive)

The trait system SHIPPED. Age + growth-event evolution is **live**: pawns carry
`age`/`birthDayOfYear`/`pendingGrowth` (`core/types/entities.ts`), aging is real (1 yr = 4 seasons =
360 days), `PawnGrowthService.processDay` fires the seasonal/birthday cadence once per in-game day
(`GameEngineImpl.processGrowth`), and `lineageGrowthEvent` (`core/Lineages.ts`) does the EVOLVE step ŌĆö
finds a staged trait, swaps it for its `evolvesTo`, applies effects ŌĆö on every growth event. The
"waits on the age system" blocker the old draft carried was **stale**; that mechanism exists.

The following refinements are **deferred but open** ŌĆö not blockers, picked up opportunistically or
folded into the specs that would need them (CREATURE-COMBAT, KINGDOMS-TRADE, SOCIAL-LAYER).

- [ ] `evolutionTrigger {minAgeYears}` **ritual gate ŌĆö SUPERSEDED, not doing.** The "deliberate,
      ritual-gated evolution" idea is already delivered by the **lineage** path (born/awaken via deed
      meters ŌåÆ grow/evolve at growth events, per [LINEAGES](LINEAGES-2026-07-09.md) /
      [LINEAGES-II](LINEAGES-II-2026-07-10.md)). The generic random-`EVOLVE_CHANCE`-at-growth-event
      path is good enough for non-lineage staged gear; no separate age/ritual gate is wanted.
- [ ] Breath `reach 3` ŌåÆ a proper AoE cone ŌĆö **deferred.** Breath weapons still fire as a single-tile
      reach-3 hit; the cone/area shape is a combat-geometry job, revisit with CREATURE-COMBAT.
- [ ] Reserved content hooks ŌĆö **scaffolding mostly in place, pawn-facing wiring deferred.** The
      creature-side `diet` field (`carnivore`/`herbivore`/`none`) drives entity hunger/hunting, and
      the graftable `spinneret` organ + Silk Spinners graft (LINEAGES-II ┬¦3) exist as a hittable,

      **silk butcher-yield** (explicit TODO in `limbmap.jsonc` ŌĆö spinneret health won't influence yield
      until butchery becomes per-organ), and `sleepless` / `beast-speech` (no code yet).
- [ ] Phase 2 kinds: `behavioral` / `needs` / `transformation` ŌĆö **flexible loose ends.** Delivered
      bespoke by LINEAGES-II (a `needs`-style blood meter `bloodthirst`, a `transformation` werewolf
